### What does the term "stale closure" mean?


- Stale means **outdated**.
- So, stale closure is when a closure gets outdated 🤧!

---


- Closure is when a function somehow **remembers its outer scope(s)** during execution, even if it was called from a totally different place from where it born 🤯.
